# SEO & Copywriting Plan - Jessica Morari

This document outlines the high-intent keywords and copywriting strategy to position Jessica Morari as the leading Life Design Coach for high-achieving women in Dubai and globally.

## 1. Target Audience
- **Primary**: High-achieving women (Executives, Entrepreneurs, Leaders).
- **Secondary**: Women in transition (Career change, relocation to Dubai, "mid-life" re-evaluation).
- **Location**: Dubai, UAE (Primary local focus) and Worldwide (Online coaching).

## 2. Priority Keywords

### High Intent (Coaching Services)
- `Life Design Coach Dubai`
- `Executive Coaching for Women Dubai`
- `Career Strategy Coach for Ambitious Women`
- `Professional Coaching for Women Dubai`
- `Leadership Coaching Dubai`

### Niche & Method-Specific
- `The Life Design Method™`
- `Redefining success for women`
- `Work-life alignment for high-achievers`
- `Dubai personal development for women`

## 3. Copywriting Strategy

### Tone & Voice
- **Empathetic & Sophisticated**: Reflecting the high-value nature of the service.
- **Direct & Action-Oriented**: Respecting the time and ambition of the clients.
- **Authoritative yet Relatable**: Combining professional ICF credentials with multi-cultural experience.

### Key Pillars & Messaging
1. **Redefining Success**: Moving beyond the "hustle" to a design that fits values.
2. **The Life Design Method™**: Emphasizing the proprietary 4-pillar framework (Vision, Alignment, Strategy, Implementation).
3. **Dubai Authority**: Leveraging the local context (high-pressure, high-reward environment) to offer specific solutions.

## 4. On-Page Implementation
- **Headings**: Ensure H1 and H2 tags include priority keywords naturally (e.g., "Life Design Coaching for the Ambitious Woman in Dubai").
- **Dynamic Content**: Ensure the FAQs generated in the AI SEO phase use these keywords to serve as "Featured Snippets".
